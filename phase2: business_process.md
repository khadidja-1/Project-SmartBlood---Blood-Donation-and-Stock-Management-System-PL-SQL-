Phase II — Business Process

This document describes the complete business process of the Smartblood system, according to the requirements of Phase II.

Objective

Model the full blood donation cycle from donor registration to delivery to the patient.

Actors

Donor

Mobile Collection Team

Lab Technician

Blood Bank Admin

Hospital

 Process Summary

The donor registers through the application and completes a health questionnaire.

The mobile collection team validates the donor’s availability and performs the blood collection.

Samples are sent to the laboratory.

The lab technician performs tests (compatibility, infectious diseases, quality).

Validated blood is stored in the blood bank.

The hospital submits a request based on blood type and availability.

The blood is assigned and delivered.
